Stage 03 — Pre-Launch

Get the infrastructure right.

Poor setup before launch is the most expensive mistake in marketing. Plan your tracking, QA, and stakeholder alignment here before it bites you live.

Ask AI to help build out your tracking plan, suggest UTM conventions, or fill in your go/no-go criteria. Try: "We use HubSpot and Salesforce together — set up tracking accordingly" or "Generate a solid go/no-go checklist for a paid social campaign."

✦ Updating fields…

Which tracking and analytics tools will be active for this campaign?

List the specific conversion events or goals you'll configure in your analytics tools.

Define the UTM structure your team will use across all campaign links.

Which areas will your QA process cover before launch?

Who needs to be briefed before launch, and how?

Will you seed early buzz or warm up audiences before the main launch?

Describe the warm-up sequence and timing if applicable.

What must be true for you to confirm the campaign is ready to launch?
