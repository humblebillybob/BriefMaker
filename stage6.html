Stage 06 — Reporting & Iteration

Close the loop. Compound the learning.

Campaigns that don't generate structured learnings just repeat themselves. Define how you'll measure, who gets the results, and how insights feed into the next cycle.

Ask AI to write your success definition, suggest a reporting structure, or build out your learnings template. Try: "Write a success definition based on our 300 lead goal and $100 CPL target" or "Suggest a learnings template for a B2B demand gen campaign."
