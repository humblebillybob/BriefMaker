Stage 04 — Launch & Activation

Go live with intent.

A coordinated multi-channel launch creates compounding impact. Define the sequence, who owns what, and what signals you'll watch in the critical first 72 hours.

Ask AI to draft your channel activation sequence, monitoring plan, or early-signal thresholds based on your budget and channels. Try: "Draft a day-by-day launch sequence for LinkedIn + email" or "What early signals should I watch for a $50K paid campaign?"

When does the campaign go live?

In what order will channels go live? Describe the sequence and timing gaps.

Platforms, initial daily budgets, and audience targeting at launch.

What organic content is scheduled for launch week?

Number of sends, segments, and approximate send times.

Any press releases, media pitches, or earned coverage planned around launch?

Who is responsible for each channel or function on launch day?

How will you monitor performance in the first 24–72 hours?

What numbers in the first 48–72 hours would trigger a pause or change of direction?

If something goes wrong at launch, who makes the call and how fast?
